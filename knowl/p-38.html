A standard deck of cards (for the purposes of this class) comprises 52 cards. Each card has a rank and a suit. The 13 possible ranks are Ace, 2–10, Jack, Queen, and King, and the 4 possible suits are hearts ♥️, diamonds ♦️, spades ♠️, and clubs ♣️. Each possible pairing of rank and suit is realized in exactly one card.
