Item b.

\(P\) is countably additive, meaning that for any countably infinite list \(A_1,A_2,\dots\) of pairwise disjoint (\(i\ne j\) implies \(A_i \cap A_j = \emptyset\)) members of \(\mathcal{F}\text{,}\) we have
\begin{equation*} P\left( \bigcup_{n \ge 1} A_i \right) = \sum_{n\ge 1} P(A_n). \end{equation*}
