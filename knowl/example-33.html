Example 2.4.3.

Suppose \(X \sim \gauss(0,1)\text{.}\) We can compute \(P(|X| \ge k)\) by direct computation:
\begin{align*} P(|X| \ge k) \amp = 2 \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}} \int_k^\infty e^{-x^2/2}\,dx \\ \amp \le \sqrt{\frac2\pi} \int_k^\infty \frac{x}{k} e^{-x^2}{2}\,dx \\ \amp = \cdots = \frac{2}{k\sqrt\pi} e^{-k^2}{2} \end{align*}
This is much smaller than \(\frac{1}{k^2}\text{,}\) the bound given by Chebyshev.
