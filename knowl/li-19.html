Item 2.
\(\{X \lt a \} = \bigcup_{b\in\mathbb{Q}, \; b \lt a} \{X \le b\}\text{,}\) since \(\mathbb{Q}\) is order-dense in \(\R\text{.}\) Since \(\mathbb{Q}\) is countable and each \(\{X \le b\}\) belongs to \(\mathcal{F}\text{,}\) we can conclude that this countable union belongs to \(\mathcal{F}\) too.
