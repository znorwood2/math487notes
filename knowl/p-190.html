Put \(A = \{X \ge a\}\text{.}\) Since \(X \ge 0\text{,}\) we have \(X \ge a 1_A\text{.}\) (Check this, perhaps by considering separately the cases \(X \le a\) and \(X \gt a\text{.}\)) Take the expectation of each side:
\begin{equation*} E(X) \ge E(a1_A) = a P(A). \end{equation*}
Rearrange to get what we want.
