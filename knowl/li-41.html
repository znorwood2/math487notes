Item 1.

Discrete random variables \(X\) and \(Y\) are independent if and only if their joint mass function is the pointwise product of their mass functions:
\begin{equation*} f_{X,Y}(x,y) = f_X(x) f_Y(y) \quad \forall x,y \end{equation*}
(This holds in Situation A of the example but not in Situation B.)
